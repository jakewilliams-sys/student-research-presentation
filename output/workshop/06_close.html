Key takeaways

Four things to remember

  • Students want to order more than they actually do.
    The gap between consideration and conversion is real — and it's driven by friction, not lack of interest.
  • Free delivery is the foundation. Everything else builds on it.
    But it has to feel genuinely free at checkout — service fees and minimum orders are eroding the promise.
  • Ordering is social. The product should be too.
    Group decisions, flatmate influence, and Friday night planning drive more orders than individual convenience.
  • Plus has the subscribers. It needs to show them the value.
    Many students already have a subscription through Prime — they just don't know what it gives them yet.
