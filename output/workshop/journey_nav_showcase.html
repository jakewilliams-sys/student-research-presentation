Journey Navigation Concepts

10 distinct visual approaches for the ordering journey stage indicator. Step through each to see how it animates between stages. Switch personas to see color adaptation.

Expanding Label

01 / 10
Current stage label grows and drops down. Previous labels shrink back. Smooth serif transition gives editorial weight to the active moment.
1 / 6

Sliding Capsule

02 / 10
A solid-color pill slides between stage positions. The capsule carries the stage name, making movement the primary communication. Decisive, kinetic.
1 / 6

Editorial Counter

03 / 10
Large serif typography announces the current stage. A minimal segment bar below tracks progress. Magazine-like, dramatic hierarchy.
1 / 6

Vertical Rail

04 / 10
Stages stacked vertically on the left edge. Current stage expands to show its name in large serif. A sidebar-style wayfinding approach.
1 / 6

Typewriter

05 / 10
The stage name types out letter by letter in large serif text. Previous stages appear as small struck-through text below. Narrative, cinematic.
1 / 6

Connected Nodes

06 / 10
Circle nodes connected by edges. Current node swells to nearly double size with a soft glow ring. Done nodes tint with the persona color.
1 / 6

Film Strip

07 / 10
Stages as frames in a horizontal strip. The current frame lifts up and enlarges, like pulling a frame from the strip. Storytelling metaphor.
1 / 6

Breadcrumb Trail

08 / 10
Chevron-separated text labels. Done stages get a strikethrough, current stage scales up in serif. Ultra-minimal, text-only approach.
1 / 6

Radial Arc

09 / 10
Stages arranged along a gentle arc. The current dot swells and its label drops down. Organic, flowing alternative to linear layouts.
1 / 6

Spotlight

10 / 10
The stage name displayed enormous and centered. An accent bar and pip row provide minimal context. Cinematic, bold, impossible to miss.
1 / 6
